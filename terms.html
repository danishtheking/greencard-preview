2. Description of Services

GreenCard Guide AI provides an agentic artificial intelligence (AI) platform designed to assist users in strategically enhancing their immigration profiles for the following U.S. immigration visa categories:

  • O-1A Visa: Individuals with extraordinary ability in sciences, education, business, or athletics
  • EB-1A Visa: Employment-based first preference for individuals with extraordinary ability
  • EB-2 NIW (National Interest Waiver): Employment-based second preference for professionals with advanced degrees or exceptional ability, with a national interest waiver

  • Profile analysis and gap identification relative to USCIS evidentiary criteria
